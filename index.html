I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Information Technology at Uppsala University. I am working on ultrasound imaging under the supervision of Orcun Goksel. My research interests are computational imaging, deep learning, signal processing, and ultrasound imaging.

I received my B.S and M.S degrees in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from Middle East Technical University (METU). In my master studies, I have worked with Figen S. Oktem. My M.S thesis focused on developing fast, efficient, and novel deep learning-based unrolled reconstruction methods for various computational imaging modalities.
